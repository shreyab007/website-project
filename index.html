About Us

Gradient is an energetic student-led community passionate about the evolving world of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. We are dedicated to nurturing creativity, teamwork, and the exchange of ideas among budding ML enthusiasts.

Our vision is to build a space where curiosity for technology thrives and innovative concepts take shape, inspiring the next wave of breakthroughs.

FAQs

Everything you need to know about joining and participating in Gradient.

Who can join Gradient?
Any student at the college with interest in AI and machine learning can join. No prior experience required. We welcome beginners and experienced builders alike.
Are there membership fees?
No. Gradient is free to join. We're funded by the college and occasional sponsorships from tech companies.
How do I stay updated?
Join our mailing list and follow our social media channels. We announce events, deadlines, and opportunities there regularly.
